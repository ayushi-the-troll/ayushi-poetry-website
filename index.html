lost packages

her mail and packages
stacked up
like she’s gone on vacation
like she’ll be back to pick it up
i bet if the postman knew
that the house is forever empty
he would throw it away
like it meant nothing
i wonder what’s in that package
what did she buy?
was it a kitchen knife,
baking supplies,
or maybe a book she wanted to read?
a book that would never make
it onto her “finally finished” list
her excitement for that gift
died with her in her sleep
she don’t need it anymore
she’s got heaven baby
but she didn’t even get to touch
the last thing she bought

never meant to be

it's been off for me
for the past few months you've been
teasing and lying
i felt it since that day
that day when i got mad
but i fought for us
i fought for us but you threw it away
you threw us away like those baby shoes
bought but never worn,
never worn, never loved, never used
but you used me as a consolation prize
a prize to lift you up
up in your dreams when she pulled you down
down into the depths of sadness
i should've broken it off then
i should've listened to myself
that we couldn't be fixed
that we were never meant to be
